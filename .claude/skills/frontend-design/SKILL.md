Before writing any code, define:
- Aesthetic direction (minimal, brutalist, editorial, playful, etc.)
- Typography scale and font choices (avoid Inter, Roboto, Arial)
- Color palette (avoid purple gradients on white)
- Animation and interaction rules

Produce production-ready code. Make it visually distinctive and memorable.
NEVER generate generic "AI slop" designs.
